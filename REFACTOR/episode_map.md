## Key Observations

1. **S01E07/E08 swap**: In the old numbering, "That Which Emerges" was S01E07 and "Learning to Be Large" was S01E08. In the new architecture, "That Which Emerges" is E08 and "The Song at the Edge of Everything" is E07. The old E07 (That Which Emerges) matches the new E08 perfectly.

2. **Cross-season duplicates**: Several episodes appear in multiple locations with slight variations:
   - `S01E09_the_sound_that_wants_to_come_home.md` exists in both root and season01/
   - `S01E11_toll_roads_and_trade_winds.md` exists in both root and season01/
   - `S01E12_wreckage_and_will.md` exists in both root and season01/
   - `S02E07_Name_with_the_Station.md` (season02) partial-matches S03E02 (Boneyard theme)
   
3. **S02E03 / S01E13**: "Meatball's Frequency" was originally S01E13 in the old system. It fits better as S02E03 in the new architecture.

4. **S03E02 / S02E07 same title**: "The Boneyard of Beautiful Things" appears in both S02E07 and S03E02 in the architecture. The season02 version (about lost frequencies) and season03 version (about Builder-era frequencies and Barry) are different episodes with the same title.

5. **Season 03 ending**: The final episode (S03E10 "The Signal Was Us All Along") is covered by three existing scripts (S03E10, S03E11, S03E12) that need to be amalgamated into one cohesive ending.
