## Common Amendments (Apply to ALL Existing Episodes)

These patterns apply across the entire library and should be addressed in every episode during amendment.

### CA-1: Clive Description — STAPLER → ROBOT 🔴 LOCKED RULE VIOLATION

**Violation**: Clive is described as a "stapler" across 16 episodes. The Locked Rules state: *"Clive is a knee-high robot with a glowing sphere head and a fedora — not a stapler."*

**Affected episodes**: S01E01, S01E04, S01E05, S01E06, S01E07, S01E08, S01E09, S01E13, S02E03, S02E05, S03E01, S03E02, S03E03, S03E07, S03E11, S03E12

**Amendment**: Replace ALL stapler references with knee-high robot description. Replace "staple patterns" communication method with an equivalent (e.g., light patterns from glowing sphere head, fedora adjustments, glow changes). Replace "springs," "binding fluid," "staple-pattern poetry/cipher" with robot-appropriate equivalents.

### CA-2: "Dimensions" → "Frequencies" 🔴 LOCKED RULE VIOLATION

**Violation**: The word "dimension" is used in-universe across 11 episodes. The Locked Rules state: *"Dimensions are called 'frequencies' — never 'dimensions,' 'planes,' or 'realms' in-universe."*

**Affected episodes (highest counts)**: S01E03 (20), S01E04 (8), S01E05 (5), S01E12 (2), S01E02 (1), S01E07 (1), S01E09 (1), S01E10 (1), S03E01 (1), S03E08 (1), S01E08 (1)

**Amendment**: Replace "dimension" / "dimensions" with "frequency" / "frequencies" in all in-universe dialogue and narration. Metadata/summary uses of "dimension" in frontmatter are acceptable if describing the concept to external readers.

### CA-3: "The Sea" → "The Space" 🔴 LOCKED RULE VIOLATION

**Violation**: "Interdimensional Sea" and "the Sea" are used pervasively across 17+ episodes. The Locked Rules state: *"The Space between frequencies is called 'the Space' or 'the Interdimensional Space' — never 'the Sea'."*

**Affected episodes**: S01E01, S01E02, S01E04, S01E07, S01E08, S01E09, S01E10, S01E11, S01E12, S01E13, S02E04, S02E05, S02E07, S02E08, S02E09, S03E10, S03E12 (and many more with "Interdimensional Sea")

**Amendment**: Replace "Interdimensional Sea" → "Interdimensional Space" and "the Sea" → "the Space" throughout all episodes. Adjust any nautical metaphors that derive from the "sea" framing (e.g., "sailed," "waves," "currents" in reference to the Space). Space vocabulary should replace nautical vocabulary where it refers to the medium between frequencies.

### CA-4: Mochi Absent from ALL Episodes 🔴 LOCKED RULE VIOLATION

**Violation**: The character "Mochi" does not appear by name in ANY of the 37 existing episodes. The Locked Rules require: *"Mochi warms at least once per episode"* and the Season 1 Architecture requires a Mochi Cue in every episode.

**Amendment**: Add Mochi to every episode. Mochi is in Pixel's jacket pocket, pulses warm near Builder-adjacent material and specific frequencies (1 and 4 in S01). Mochi does NOT speak, does NOT have complex emotions. Mochi is warm, simple, holds one enormous secret (a data crystal / frequency atlas). Each episode needs at minimum one warming/glow/pulse beat.

### CA-5: A1 Coffee Flavor Missing in Many Episodes 🔴 LOCKED RULE VIOLATION

**Violation**: The Locked Rules require: *"A1's coffee flavor is noted in every scene where A1 is present."* Multiple episodes feature A1 prominently with zero coffee/espresso mentions.

**Episodes with A1 present but no coffee**: S01E12, S02E03, S02E06, S02E07, S02E08, S03E02

**Amendment**: Add A1 coffee flavor notes in every episode where A1 is present. Coffee flavor should reflect A1's emotional/processing state (e.g., burnt static when scared, rich chocolate when confident, unfamiliar ancient flavors near Builder material).

### CA-6: Ephergent Frequency Beat Missing

**Violation**: The Season Architectures require the Ephergent Frequency to be heard briefly in every episode. Many existing episodes do not include this.

**Amendment**: Add an Ephergent Frequency beat to every episode — a hum in the walls, static under a broadcast, a note in A1's engine, a melody Meatball tilts his head toward.

### CA-7: Barry Trail — Needs Seeding in S01, Escalation in S02/S03

**Violation**: Barry references are present in some episodes but inconsistent with the season architectures' required escalation pattern. Some episodes characterize Barry as "disappeared" or "lost" rather than the Locked Rule framing (*"Barry Kowalski is alive. He is in the Wellspring. He left a trail."*).

**Affected episodes**: S01E01 ("Barry Kowalski disappeared decades ago" — should be reframed as "went into the Space/followed a signal"), S03E12 (memorial language — should be reframed as alive/deliberate)

**Amendment**: Ensure all Barry references frame him as alive and deliberate, not lost or tragic. His notes, when found, should be methodical, not dramatic. His trail is intentional.
