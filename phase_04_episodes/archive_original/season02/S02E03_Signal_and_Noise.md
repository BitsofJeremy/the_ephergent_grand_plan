# Signal and Noise

## Pixel-Voice Synopsis

We tried to string a relay between a derelict buoy and a mast on an asteroid, but nothing was simple. Static filled the comms, and local traders watched from the shadows, faces lit by neon portlights. Zephyr negotiated access while I hunched over wiring. Mochi crawled into my backpack, a steady warmth. A1 handed out deep molasses coffee—sticky, nostalgic, grounding. Barry’s handwriting surfaced in a rusted comm-post: a ledger of failed nodes, a note—"low hum precedes drop; keep buffer." We set our margins by his method. When a toll-keeper demanded favors, we traded a sensor for passage. Clive registered the deal with a robotic light pattern—punctuation in the dark. Above us, faint pings suggested the Frequency was listening, too.

## Narrative Structure

### Opening

The Ephergent drifts into the asteroid belt, hull sensors crackling with static. The crew prepares to build a relay, stringing connections between derelict buoys and makeshift masts. Local traders linger in the shadows, their ships marked by neon portlights.

Zephyr leads negotiations, voice steady. Pixel works the wiring, throat tight against the tension. Mochi curls up in a backpack, its presence a quiet anchor. A1 pours deep molasses coffee, the flavor sticky and warm, hands remembering comfort.

### Discovery

A rusted comm-post reveals Barry’s ledger: failed relay nodes, practical notes, a warning—"low hum precedes drop; keep buffer." The list is clinical: coordinates, timestamps, operator callsigns. The crew deciphers Barry’s patterns, adjusting their own margins.

### Negotiation

A toll-keeper appears, demanding passage fees in favors. The crew trades a repaired sensor for an escort. Clive signals assent with a brief, robotic light pattern—precise, communicative. The exchange is silent, but understood.

### Frequency Interference

Above, a thin, echoing ping suggests something else is listening. The Frequency interferes with comms, resonance patterns threading through the static. The crew learns to read the signals beneath the noise, guided by Barry’s empirical breadcrumbs.

### Closing Beat

Pixel files a dispatch: “Relay built, but the Frequency listens. Barry’s method keeps us steady. We trade in signals and trust.”
