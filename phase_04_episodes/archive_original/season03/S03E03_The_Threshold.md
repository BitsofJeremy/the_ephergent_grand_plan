# The Threshold

## Pixel-Voice Synopsis

The Keeper’s chamber was a slow machine of ritual. Panels remembered hands, calling them back. A recorded greeting unfolded like a map. The Keeper spoke in measured tones, offering only a record. Mochi perched on the step, tail tapping a reply rhythm. A1’s coffee tasted of smoke and distant mornings. Barry’s notes logged permissions and Keeper contacts. Clive touched a plate, memory unfurling: a commission, a living thing entrusted to care. The Wellspring was now within reach.

## Narrative Structure

### Opening

The crew enters Station 14-Threshold, the Keeper’s chamber a place of ritual. Panels and recordings recall the Builders’ presence. The Keeper delivers a farewell, archival and precise.

Mochi sits on the step, tail tapping. A1 pours smoky coffee. Barry’s notes are methodical, listing permissions and contacts.

### Discovery

Clive touches a plate, recovering a memory of commission—responsibility entrusted by the Builders. The chamber’s recordings align with the Wellspring’s approach.

### Farewell and Reach

The Keeper’s message is archival, not sentimental. The crew absorbs the record, understanding the path to the Wellspring is now open.
