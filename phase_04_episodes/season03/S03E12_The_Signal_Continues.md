# The Signal Continues

## Pixel-Voice Synopsis

The transmission left the ship like a held breath released. For a long moment, nothing happened. Then a ripple of acknowledgements returned—small, careful replies from frequencies they had met and places only touched by name. Mochi slept under a console as replies came in, pings matching its tail rhythm. A1’s coffee was thin and black, taken without ceremony. Barry sat with his notebook, adding data as replies streamed in. The future smelled of coffee and solder. The crew watched a pulse on the long-range array that felt like possibility. The signal continued; the rest would be tending.

## Narrative Structure

### Opening

The crew sends the Builder message, filtered through their lived experience. The ship waits in quiet anticipation.

### Replies

Acknowledgements return from across the frequencies. Mochi sleeps, tail in rhythm with the pings. A1 drinks thin black coffee. Barry records incremental data.

### Future

The crew stands at the rail, watching the array. The future is open, possibility in the air.

### Closing Beat

Pixel files a dispatch: “The signal continues. Replies arrive. The Frequency is a living exchange, and the future is open.”
