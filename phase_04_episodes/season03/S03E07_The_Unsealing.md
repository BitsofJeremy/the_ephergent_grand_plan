# The Unsealing

## Pixel-Voice Synopsis

Public attention brought papers and petitioners. Baron Klaus’s emissary arrived with a polite demand: share the Builders’ work or be contained. Nocturne politics preferred archival control. Mochi sat at the threshold, watching as emissaries debated. A1 slipped a maple-smoked coffee to a junior archivist. Barry’s notes hinted at records that could be freed with the right signatures. The confrontation unfolded in bureaucratic motions. The crew freed some records, held others for protection—a compromise that left everyone uneasy but moving.

### Opening

Bureaucratic maneuvers unfold: forms, contacts, quiet strategies. The crew must choose what to release and what to protect.

### Compromise

Pixel files a dispatch: “The Archive is unsealed, but not emptied. Choices are made in the open, and the Frequency shifts.”
