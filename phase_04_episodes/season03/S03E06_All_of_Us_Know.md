# All of Us Know

## Pixel-Voice Synopsis

We sat in a circle, each translating a line of the Builder fragment. Every voice added a piece—imperfect, cumulative. Mochi moved from lap to lap, resting its head, tail thumping in quiet approval. A1’s coffee was cinnamon-flecked, lending a warm rhythm. Barry’s fragment became a chorus when layered with our interpretations. Clive’s contribution was a pattern of light pulses, suggesting maintenance cadence. For a moment, the fragment felt like a shared artifact.

## Narrative Structure

### Opening

The crew gathers for a translation session, each member offering their interpretation of the Builder fragment.

Mochi provides comfort, lap-hopping. A1 pours cinnamon-flecked coffee. Barry’s notes become a chorus.

### Discovery

Clive’s light pulses add a machine beat to the human phrases. The group stitches together meaning, creating a living broadcast.

### Reflection

The session emphasizes communal interpretation over final answers. The crew feels united in their understanding of “Ephergent.”

### Closing Beat

Pixel files a dispatch: “Each voice adds to the Frequency. The fragment is ours, for a moment, and we are Ephergent.”
