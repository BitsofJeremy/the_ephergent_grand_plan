## Motivation
The Patron's motivations remain one of the season's central mysteries. They knew precisely what lay in that Verdanya temple — not just "a Builder device" but specifically Mochi and Clive. They knew the site well enough to provide exact coordinates. They had the resources to deposit significant payment upfront with no negotiation, and the technical capability to cover their digital tracks completely. Most tellingly, they had the political capital to bend the Green Faith's sacred site protocols, something the Rootminders "don't do for outsiders. Ever."

The job itself was unusual: no introduction, no negotiation, no explanation. Just coordinates, payment, and three words: "Retrieve the device." When Pixel tried to trace the sender, she hit nothing but dead ends. This wasn't amateur hour — this was someone with serious resources and serious secrets.

The question that haunts Pixel: Did the Patron know what Mochi would do? Did they know about Clive? Did they understand what they were setting in motion? And most unsettling of all — are they watching to see what happens next?

## Relationship to Team
- **To Pixel**: The Patron's relationship with Pixel is entirely one-sided and defined by absence. They chose her specifically for this retrieval, which suggests they know her reputation, her skills, and possibly her character. They trusted her with something monumentally important but gave her no context. Pixel is equal parts grateful (the job changed her life) and suspicious (nobody does this without a reason). She refers to them as "The Patron" with a mixture of respect and wariness.
- **Dynamic**: Ghost in the machine. The Patron's presence is felt through their absence — every time the team encounters something connected to Mochi or Clive's origins, Pixel wonders if the Patron knew. Every time they face danger, she wonders if it was anticipated. The Patron is both potential ally (they put Mochi in Pixel's hands) and potential threat (they're clearly operating with information nobody else has).
- **Key Quote**: *"Three words in my inbox. 'Retrieve the device.' No name. No explanation. Just coordinates and payment already sitting in my account. I should've walked away. But whoever sent that message... they knew I wouldn't."* — Pixel, Episode 1
