## Motivation
Torque's primary loyalty is to her guild and its people. She sees Pillars through the lens of resources and obligations — guild contracts, Senate quotas, employment for her workers. Not callous, but practical. When confronted with evidence of Pillar sabotage, she pivots quickly from salvage politics to collective defense, showing leadership instinct beneath the pragmatism.
