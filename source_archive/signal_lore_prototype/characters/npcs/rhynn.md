## Motivation
Survival. Rhynn found something too big and too dangerous for a solo salvager. Put out a call for highest bidder, then went dark. When The Quiet showed up, her situation went from profitable to lethal. After the Keeper is saved, she shows the deeper Driftborn understanding — some things are respected not because you understand them, but because the ice demands it.
