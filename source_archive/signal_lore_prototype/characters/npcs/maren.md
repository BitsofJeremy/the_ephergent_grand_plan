## Voice Description (TTS)
- **Tone**: Quiet conviction. Measured certainty that softens into uncertainty as her worldview shifts. "Mercy" is her word — she believes in what she's doing.
- **Pacing**: Deliberate, careful — every word chosen. Speeds up slightly when challenged or when new possibilities emerge.
- **Accent**: Educated, multi-regional — The Quiet draws from all regions
- **Key Qualities**: Can say "Or repeat it" with enough weight to stop an argument. Her shift from antagonist to ally isn't a collapse — it's a genuine reassessment. "The Quiet will help" carries the authority of someone committing her entire organization.
- **Reference**: Think a resistance leader who discovers the war might have a third option — conviction redirected, not abandoned

## Political/Religious Affiliation
- **Political**: The Quiet (leader) — decades of clandestine work to prevent what they see as catastrophic Signal collapse. Anti-institutional, operates across all regions.
- **Religious**: Post-religious — The Quiet's philosophy transcends regional faiths. They believe the Builders' legacy is dangerous, not sacred.

## Motivation
Originally: "Mercy" — putting the Builders to final rest, letting Aethon learn to live without the Pillars. She genuinely believes awakening Builder consciousness risks catastrophe. Her shift comes when she sees a third option — not wake them or kill them, but stabilize them. She commits The Quiet's resources because "Maybe it's time we tried preventing catastrophe by moving forward instead of holding back."
