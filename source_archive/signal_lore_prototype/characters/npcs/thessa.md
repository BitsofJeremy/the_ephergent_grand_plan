## Motivation
Guardian of the Garden of Forgotten Hours — a place that blooms once every fifty years, sharing preserved memories through living cells. Thessa has witnessed multiple bloomings and each time forgets how much light there was. Her purpose is to help others see what was, so they understand what could be again.
