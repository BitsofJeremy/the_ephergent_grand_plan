## Political/Religious Affiliation
- **Political**: Listener authority (not aligned with Blood Council or Resonant warlords). Operates within Nocturnis's power structure but independent enough to invite outsiders.
- **Religious**: Deep Choir — "We are pauses between notes—temporary arrangements of frequency in The Song. To preserve memory is to honor the verse that came before."

## Motivation
Preservation of acoustic memory — the Listeners' sacred duty. Siran called for outside help only because centuries of slow song-death finally threatened total loss. She weighs every decision against tradition and necessity, and chose necessity. Her fury at The Quiet's infiltration is personal — they corrupted her institution from within.
