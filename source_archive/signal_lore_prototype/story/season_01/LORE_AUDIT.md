# Season 01 Lore Integration Audit

**Purpose**: Cross-reference all 12 episodes against the new lore documents to identify gaps, contradictions, and opportunities for deepening.

**Lore References**:
- `lore/the_builders_origin.md` — Creator-level truth (Earth colonists, terraforming, the Upload)
- `lore/politics/politics_of_aethon.md` — Five political systems
- `lore/religion/religion_of_aethon.md` — Five faiths + universal Builder reverence
- `lore/cultures/cultures_of_aethon.md` — Daily life, food, art, customs per region
- `lore/history/history_of_aethon.md` — Seven-era timeline
- `lore/the_pillars_and_signal.md` — Pillar mechanics, Signal behavior
- `lore/world_of_aethon.md` — Geography, governance overview
- `lore/glossary_and_terms.md` — Terminology reference

---

## Audit Categories

For each episode, evaluate these five dimensions:

### P — Political Context
Who governs this area? What power dynamics should the characters encounter? Are there authority figures, jurisdictional tensions, or political obstacles that would realistically exist?

### R — Religious Practices
What faith dominates this region? Would characters witness prayers, rituals, religious architecture, or belief-driven behavior? Would religious attitudes shape how NPCs react to the team?

### C — Cultural Details
What greetings, food, language, social norms, naming conventions, art, or music would ground this scene? What does daily life look like here?

### B — Builder Breadcrumbs
Per `the_builders_origin.md` "Clues to Plant" section — are there opportunities to seed hints that the Builders were engineers, not mystics? That the Pillars are machines? That the food came from elsewhere?

### X — Contradictions
Does anything in the episode directly conflict with the new lore? Terminology, geography, political structures, religious practices, or historical claims that now need correcting.

---

## Scoring Guide

For each category, rate the episode:

- **0 — Absent**: No mention at all. Needs new content.
- **1 — Gestural**: Brief, generic references. Could be deepened significantly.
- **2 — Present**: Meaningful references that align with lore. Minor additions needed.
- **3 — Rich**: Deeply integrated, feels like a lived-in world. No changes needed.
