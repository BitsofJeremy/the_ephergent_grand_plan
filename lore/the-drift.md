The Drift is not a villain. It is entropy — the natural tendency of frequencies to drift apart over time, like radio stations slowly detuning. The space between worlds gets wider. Signals get fainter. Dimensions that were once neighbors become strangers, then memories, then nothing.

Left unchecked, every frequency will eventually fade. Not with a bang — with a whisper.

The crew fights The Drift by broadcasting. By documenting. By connecting worlds that are forgetting each other exists. Every transmission carries enough frequency resonance to temporarily stabilize a fading frequency. When people hear The Ephergent, their world gets a little more real.
