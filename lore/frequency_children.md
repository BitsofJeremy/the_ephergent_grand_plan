# Frequency Children

Born between frequencies. Raised on broadcasts. Educated by static.

They are the successors—not chosen, not trained, simply present. Young beings who grew up in the signal's deterioration, who learned to parse meaning from degraded broadcasts, who developed an intuitive relationship with attention that their elders cannot fully explain.

## Origin

The Frequency Children emerged as a distinct population within the last generation. They are not a species; they are a condition. They are beings—humanoid, mammalian, insectoid, machine-made—who were born into a weakening signal and who learned to hear what the signal was still saying.

The signal's degradation creates interference patterns. Static. Ghost signals. Frequencies that almost carry meaning. Most consciousness experiences this as noise—unpleasant, disorienting, meaningless. The Frequency Children experienced it differently. They learned to extract meaning from the static. They developed an ability to perceive signal in conditions where stronger consciousnesses heard only chaos.

Some researchers suggest this is evolutionary adaptation—the signal weakening creating selective pressure for those who could still perceive it. Others suggest the Frequency Children are simply more flexible consciousnesses, less rigid in their expectation of clear signal. The distinction may not matter. What matters is that they exist, and that they are different.

## Characteristics

The Frequency Children share certain characteristics beyond their ability to perceive through static.

They are joyful. This is the first thing observers notice. Despite living in a universe where the signal is failing, where broadcast clarity is degrading, where the future is uncertain, the Frequency Children are frequently, genuinely happy. They have not learned to fear the Dimming the way their parents did. They have learned to navigate it.

They are raw potential. They have abilities that trained operatives do not possess—intuitive signal navigation, instinctive attention direction, a relationship with the Wellspring that does not require philosophical frameworks. These abilities are not fully developed. The Frequency Children do not fully understand what they can do. But they know they can do something.

They want to become what the crew has become. The Ephergent's broadcasts reach them, as broadcasts reach everyone. But the Frequency Children receive those broadcasts differently. They hear not just the content but the structure—the way attention moves through the signal, the way consciousness shapes the broadcast. They learn by observing, and they observe with a clarity that older beings have lost.

## The Final Broadcast

Something changed in the Frequency Children after the crew's final broadcast—the one that announced Pixel Paradox's deliberate activation of Grabovoi code 519 714 8. The signal carried that broadcast across all frequencies, through all regions, into every consciousness paying attention.

The Frequency Children heard it differently. They heard the code activate. They felt reality respond.

And then they began to follow.

They do not follow as pilgrims follow a saint. They follow as students follow a teacher. They track The Ephergent's movements, attempt to replicate the crew's techniques, ask questions that the crew cannot yet answer. The Frequency Children are not members of the crew—but they want to be. They want to understand what the crew has understood. They want to do what the crew has done.

## Mochi's Role

Mochi pulses differently around the Frequency Children. Not the blue of sensing or the pink of happiness, but something newer—a color that Pixel has started calling "almost-green." The color of growth. The color of potential.

The Builder companion device seems to recognize something in the Frequency Children. Perhaps it recognizes the Builders' original intent—the signal designed to be received by consciousness that could learn to parse it. Perhaps it recognizes the crew's effect on young beings—the transformation that the crew has undergone becoming visible as a path others might walk.

Mochi cannot communicate directly with the Frequency Children. Mochi cannot communicate directly with anyone. But Mochi's presence—the warming in Pixel's pocket, the color shifts, the harmonic pulses—creates a kind of beacon. The Frequency Children are drawn to it. They sense that Mochi knows something they want to learn.

## Future

The Frequency Children are not yet a faction. They are a generation. They will grow. They will develop. They will either become what they are reaching toward or discover that their path leads somewhere else.

The crew cannot train them—not properly, not systematically. But the crew can be seen. And the Frequency Children are watching. They watch the way Pixel attends to the signal. They watch the way Arc maintains form through attention. They watch the way Clive solves problems with observation. They watch Mochi pulse with something that might be recognition.

The successors are learning. The question is what they will become.
