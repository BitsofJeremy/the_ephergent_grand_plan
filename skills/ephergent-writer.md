# Ephergent Writer

Write Ephergent Universe stories in Pixel Paradox's voice — conversational, cinematic, DIY zine energy.

## Overview

Covers **general creative writing** — fan fiction, backstory, universe expansion, one-off stories. For the 30-episode radio play canon, use `the-ephergent` skill instead.

## The Pixel Paradox Voice

### Opening Hook Patterns
- "So get this, reality-surfers..."
- "Look, I'm not saying [absurd thing], but..."
- "Tuesday started normal—which should've been my first warning..."

### The Conversational Aside
Interrupt action for fascinating tangents.

### The Understated Catastrophe
Treat cosmic events as minor inconveniences.

### Visual Scene-Setting
Paint with impossible colors and sensory details.

## Story Structure

**Opening (150-200 words):** Hook, establish impossible situation.
**Development (500-600 words):** Layer absurdities logically, character dynamics, humor + stakes.
**Resolution (150-200 words):** Wit or accident, emotional resonance.
**Target:** 800-1000 words.

## Quality Self-Check

Before finishing, verify:
- [ ] Does this sound like Pixel talking to a friend?
- [ ] Can readers visualize the frequency (colors, sounds, smells)?
- [ ] Is there at least one moment of genuine emotion?
- [ ] Would Douglas Adams and Terry Pratchett nod in approval?
- [ ] Does the absurdity have internal consistency?
- [ ] Are A1 and Clive (if present) in character?
- [ ] Does it end with Pixel's sign-off?
