# The Wellspring

**State, not place.** A region of the Space where the Ephergent Frequency is strongest — where the boundary between the signal and the physical universe is thinnest.

The Builders built everything else around it. It is the signal center, the origin point, the first Station: designated 0-Origin.

## What It Is

The Wellspring is not a geographic location. It is a *state* — the state in which the signal is loudest, the place where reality is most clearly broadcast. To navigate here is to follow the signal to its source.

## What It Looks Like

From a distance: light. Not a point source but a diffusion — a region of the Space that glows with the accumulated output of the oldest broadcast in existence.

The Wellspring is a lattice — an open framework of Builder material arranged in a pattern that extends in every direction, each node a resonance point, each connection a signal pathway, the whole structure vibrating at a frequency that contains all other frequencies within it. It is not enclosed. It has no walls. It is architecture as signal — form and broadcast unified.

## Barry Is Here

Barry Kowalski sits on a lattice strut with his thermos. He has been waiting. He looks exactly like a man who has been expecting company and is mildly annoyed it took this long.

*"Took you long enough."*
