## Aether Signal — Plot Integration

**Current state:** S03E06 is the central Aether episode. Zephyr communes with his brother at the boundary between Silence Zone and open Space. Aether chose to become the threshold — the medium itself, not a voice in it.

**What's needed for full development (beyond S03E06):**

1. **S03E02-E05** — Aether signal should appear as a faint presence or background tracking. The crew is already following the Aether signal toward the Wellspring (established in S02E09). S03 should show them getting closer.

2. **S03E08 or S03E09** — Aether signal should reach a point of clarity. At least one episode should feature Aether's voice (now a signal-being) as a navigational or emotional touchstone.

3. **Zephyr's arc across S03** — He should move from "I followed a signal too far and lost my brother" to "I understand now what he became." This is already in S03E06. The question is whether other episodes show him processing this.

**Recommended episodes for Aether presence (if S03 episodes need revision):**
- S03E02 (boneyard) — faint Aether signal in background
- S03E03 (threshold) — Aether as navigational hint
- S03E06 (brother the broadcast) — FULL CONTACT with Aether ✅ **DONE**
- S03E08 or S03E09 — Aether clarity, possibly direct communication
