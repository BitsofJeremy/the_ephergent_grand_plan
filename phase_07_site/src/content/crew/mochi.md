# Mochi

Mochi is a data crystal entity, approximately the size of a tennis ball, that lives in Pixel's jacket pocket. She glows — consistently, warmly, a low amber luminescence that pulses gently in response to the frequencies around her. She has never spoken a word. She has never needed to.

Mochi is a Keeper — one of a class of Builder entities designed to carry information across the vast distances of the Space. She was not always small enough to fit in a pocket. She compressed herself over centuries, conserving energy, waiting. Clive made her. Calibrated her crystal. Put the frequency map inside her. Promised he would come back.

It took him eight hundred years. She waited.

## What She Does

Mochi glows. This is not a simple output — it is a language.

- **Warm amber**: Normal, content, present
- **Deep green**: Near Verdantia, near the Root Network, near living systems
- **Bright gold**: Near Luminara, near joy crystals, near emotional resonance
- **Dim, almost dark**: Grief-adjacent, silence-adjacent, loss-adjacent
- **Intensifying**: Near the Wellspring, near signal concentration, near what she has been carrying

Mochi responds to the crew's emotional states without analyzing them. She warms when Pixel is calm. She dims when the crew encounters a Silence Zone. She intensifies when the Ephergent Frequency grows stronger. She has been doing this since Season 1, and the crew is only now beginning to understand what she has been carrying.

## The Map

Mochi contains a frequency map — hundreds of frequencies, worlds that could exist, worlds that did exist and faded. The Builders' complete atlas, compressed into a crystal small enough to fit in a pocket.

The map opens fully at the Wellspring. Eight hundred years of carrying, and the carrying was enough.

## Mochi Never Speaks

This is not a limitation. This is a design choice. Mochi communicates through warmth, through pulse, through presence. She does not need words. She has never needed words. She has been carrying the map since before the crew existed, and she has been waiting with the patience of something that knows time differently than people do.

She glows when she is found. She glows brighter when she is recognized. She has never once needed to explain herself.
