# The Edge

**Frequency 5** — Barely coherent. Reality is thinner here. Strange things happen.

The Edge exists at the boundary of reality itself — a cosmic construction site where nascent universes bubble into existence and collapsing micro-realities compose symphonies of destruction. Colors here don't have names because they haven't been invented yet. The ground is liquid. The sky folds. Geometry is a suggestion that reality politely declines.

## Signal

Chaos tuned to wonder — a roaring, shimmering wall of sound that contains fragments of every frequency that ever was or could be. Listening to it is like hearing every radio station simultaneously. Most people hear noise. A1 hears music. Om Kai hears the universe thinking.

## The Dimming

The Edge, being the most unstable frequency by nature, absorbed the Drift's effects differently. Where Nocturne grew quieter and Cogsworth started skipping, The Edge simply became *more itself* — thinner, stranger, less predictable. The Nursery Fields produce fewer new worlds. The Wellspring Approach feels farther away. Reality stress-fractures multiply. The universe is still creating, but slower, quieter.

## Stability

Volatile but enduring. The Edge cannot "fail" in the way other frequencies can because it was never fully coherent to begin with. Its stability is paradoxical: it persists because instability is its nature. But the character of that instability is changing. Less creation, more dissolution.
