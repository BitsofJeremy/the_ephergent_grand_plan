# Cogsworth Cogitarium

**Frequency 3** — Mechanical, clockwork, obsessed with precision and function.

Brass, copper, and polished mahogany. The entire frequency is a sprawling clockwork city — gears the size of buildings interlock with delicate mechanisms no larger than a watch. Steam curls from a thousand vents. The air carries aromas of roasted clockwork nuts, burnt sugar, and polished brass. Everything ticks. Everything turns. Everything is precisely on time.

## Signal

Metronomic — a precise, clicking rhythm underlaid with the hiss of steam and the whir of gears. It sounds like a clock shop at midnight: every timepiece slightly different, all somehow in sync. The signal is hypnotic. Spend too long listening and you start walking in time with it.

## The Dimming

Cogsworth's clocks are the canary in the cosmic coal mine. When the Drift intensifies, Cogsworth feels it first. Gears skip. Springs unwind without cause. The Grand Orrery wobbles. Cascade failures ripple through the frequency: a single gear out of sync can unravel an entire district.

## Stability

Deteriorating measurably. Cogsworth is the only frequency that can quantify its own decline — the Grand Orrery tracks frequency coherence with mechanical exactitude, and the numbers are not encouraging.
