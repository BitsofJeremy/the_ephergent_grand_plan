# Verdantia

**Frequency 4** — Living, overgrown, organic. Everything is alive and growing into everything else.

Every shade of green, from pale jade to deep emerald, accented by bioluminescent fungi pulsing in soft blues, golds, and magentas. Architecture is grown, not built — towering ancient trees form living skyscrapers with windows of translucent leaf-membrane. The ground is a spongy, mossy carpet. The air smells of rain, growing things, and something sweet you can never quite identify.

## Signal

Organic — a low, thrumming pulse like a heartbeat heard through the earth. Layered: the rustle of leaves, the creak of growing wood, the subsonic hum of the root network thinking. The signal feels alive in a way no other frequency does.

## The Dimming

Verdantia's collective consciousness gives it unique resilience against the Drift — as long as the root network holds, the frequency sustains. But some roots have gone quiet. Entire branches of the network whisper things the Canopy Parliament does not want to hear. Verdantia is not crumbling. It is disconnecting.

## Stability

Resilient but diminishing. The network's quiet zones are expanding. Communication between distant nodes takes longer. The collective consciousness that defines Verdantia is becoming less collective.
