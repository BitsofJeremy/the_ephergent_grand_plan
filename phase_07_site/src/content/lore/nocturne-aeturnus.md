# Nocturne Aeturnus

**Frequency 2** — Dark, gothic, permanent twilight, beautiful in a mournful way.

Deep indigo, purple, and midnight blue. The air is thick with petrichor and ancient stone. Gothic spires rise from mist-shrouded valleys. Candlelight flickers in windows of estates built from solidified emotion. Everything here feels like the most beautiful moment of dusk, stretched into eternity.

## Signal

A low, resonant chord — like a cello played in a cathedral. Melancholy and gorgeous. When you tune in, you feel it in your chest before you hear it. The signal carries emotional weight; receivers sometimes weep without knowing why.

## The Dimming

Nocturne Aeturnus is particularly vulnerable to the Drift. When the Builder Stations began failing, emotional resonance across the frequency weakened. Emotion crystallization — the economic engine and cultural foundation of Nocturne society — became unstable. Crystals crack without warning. Moods become unpredictable. The Sorrowful Moon flickers. Some nights it dims entirely.

## Stability

Fragile. The Drift hits emotional frequencies hardest because their coherence depends on resonance — and resonance is exactly what fading Stations can no longer sustain. The crystals that form now are smaller, less complex, more prone to fracture.
