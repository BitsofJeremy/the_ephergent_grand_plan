# Transmissions

The Ephergent's broadcast log — updates from the crew, recorded as they happen.

These transmissions are the show. The crew's voice, reaching out across frequencies, telling the story of what they're finding, what they're feeling, what they're learning.

---

## What You'll Find Here

**Mission Logs** — Pixel's transmissions from the field. The voice of the show, broadcasting to stay sane and to document the impossible.

**Crew Notes** — Observations, discoveries, and reflections from across the crew. Clive's measured assessments. Nano's technical assessments. Om Kai's meditations.

**Frequency Reports** — Updates on the stability of the five known frequencies and the Spaces between them. The crew's ongoing assessment of the Drift's progress.

**Discovery Archives** — Expanded entries on the strange, beautiful, and dangerous things the crew encounters in the Space between worlds.
