## SEASON DISTRIBUTION PHILOSOPHY

**Season 1 — Awareness:** Characters encounter code-like patterns without recognizing them as deliberate sequences. The universe speaks in numbers; the crew learns to hear the grammar.

**Season 2 — Understanding:** Codes are identified, studied, and first used deliberately. The crew discovers that numerical sequences are frequency coordinates with specific effects.

**Season 3 — Mastery:** Codes become both tool and threat. Code storms emerge. Deliberate weaponization occurs. The resolution requires the crew to understand codes deeply enough to choose which ones to broadcast.
