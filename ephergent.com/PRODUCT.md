# PRODUCT.md

name: Ephergent — ephergent.com
register: brand

## Product purpose
Ephergent is a games-as-story platform and narrative hub showcasing episodic, small-footprint browser and downloadable games tied to a shared sci-fi universe. It presents playable stories, lore, and creative tools that invite curiosity and repeated exploration.

## Core users
- Players who love short, high-quality narrative games and serialized fiction
- Fans of the Ephergent universe (readers, worldbuilders)
- Creators looking to publish short interactive experiences (indie-friendly)
- Community members who follow episodic releases and lore updates

## Tone & Voice
- Playful, curious, and slightly uncanny
- Warmly experimental — polished craft, not corporate polish
- Accessible and clear; playful without being silly

## Brand principles
1. Story-first: design choices serve narrative clarity and emotional beats.
2. Lightweight deliveries: 15–20MB target per game/artifact; fast initial load.
3. Inclusive: accessibility & performance are first-class constraints.
4. Distinctive: avoid category-default palettes or hackneyed genre tropes.
5. Open invitation: low friction for creators to contribute and players to join.

## Success metrics
- Weekly active players and repeat visits per episode
- Creator submissions and published micro-games
- Average load time and first-paint metrics under 3s for common connections
- Accessibility score >= 90 (automated + manual checks)

## Anti-references
- Corporate SaaS marketing pages (cold, corporate blue gradients)
- Hero-metric dashboards and identical card grids
- Dark neon-on-black “crypto” aesthetics

## Constraints
- Static-first site (Astro); deployable to static hosts
- 15–25MB per game/art asset limit
- Must support progressive enhancement for low-end devices
