The Interdimensional Sea is not empty space. It is the quantum static between frequencies — the hiss you hear between radio stations, expanded into an ocean of impossible depth. It has currents (probability flows), weather (frequency storms), and ecosystems of entities that evolved to live between worlds.

Only The Ephergent can truly navigate it. Phase-shifters hop between known stations. A1 *flies* through the static between them, reading the signal the way a navigator reads the void.
