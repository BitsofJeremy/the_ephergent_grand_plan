The crew has found an echo of Nocturne Aeturnus — a mirror frequency where every emotion is inverted. Mourners laugh until they collapse. Weddings end in grief. The crystals fall upward. The locals don't know anything is wrong.

*The Laughing Funeral* is a quiet, contemplative visual novel. There is no fail state. There is only what you choose to say, and what you choose to carry when you leave.
