In this episode: The Ephergent drifts where no ship should go — into the Breach, a seven-hundred-year-old graveyard of signals and wreckage. The captain faces an impossible choice: stop to search for survivors among the scattered remains of Station Prometheus-7, or trust that survival means never stopping at all. The Signal reminds us that captains are forged not in the obvious moments, but in the fraction of a second when looking away would be easier.
