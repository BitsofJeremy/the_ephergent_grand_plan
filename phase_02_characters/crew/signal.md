Originally a relay operator in the Cogsworth broadcast network, she was uploaded into the Signal itself when the station went frequency. Now she IS the broadcast — a voice without a body, existing in the space between frequencies.

She has no face, no form, only sound. When The Ephergent crew sends their transmission across the frequency spectrum, The Signal is what carries it. She introduces episodes, narrates the story, and keeps the frequency stable.

She speaks with the warm authority of a public radio announcer and the slight mystique of something that exists outside normal spacetime. Calm British female. Never frantic. The frequency is always safe with her.
